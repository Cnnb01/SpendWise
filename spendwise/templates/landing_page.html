Welcome to Spendwise!

We help you take control of your finances with ease

Budget creation

Create and customize budgets for various purposes. Whether it's for monthly expenses, a vacation, or a special project, our app allows you to set up budgets tailored to your needs, helping you manage your finances effectively.

Expense tracking

Log and categorize your expenses with ease. Our app provides a user-friendly interface to keep track of your spending habits, allowing you to see where your money goes and helping you make informed financial decisions.

Real-time sync

Access your expense data from anywhere, anytime. Our real-time sync feature ensures that your data is always up-to-date across all your devices, giving you peace of mind and accurate trackingon the go.

The inspiration behind the project

The idea for this expense tracking app came from our personal struggles with managing finances. We found it challenging to keep track of my expenses and stick to a budget. Therefore we aimed to create a simple yet powerful tool for budgeting and tracking expenses.
